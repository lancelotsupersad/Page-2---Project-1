Project Overview

Startups and companies grow all the time, and with that growth comes a larger need for organization and communication. Individuals in charge of managing large teams of people may find it difficult to manage and communicate with a large number of employees. With my staff scheduling app, individuals will be able to communicate and schedule their employees efficiently and effectively.

My first official UX project is a staff scheduling app that focuses on optimizing the scheduling experience for managers in the workplace. This project was completed for the Google UX design certificate I began in the summer, and has taught me the fundamentals of the UX design framework.
